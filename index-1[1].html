📚 Competitive Exams Guide

🔴 SSC Exams (Staff Selection Commission)

📸 SSC Photograph

  • File Size: 20 KB – 50 KB
  • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm
  • Background: White or light-colored
  • Note: Recent color photo, no spectacles/cap, frontal view. Some exams require live photos.

✍️ SSC Signature

  • File Size: 10 KB – 20 KB
  • Dimensions: 4.0 cm × 2.0 cm
  • Format: JPG/JPEG
  • Note: Signed on white paper with a black ink pen.

🚂 Railway Exams (RRB / RRC)

📸 Railway Photograph

  • File Size: 20 KB – 50 KB
  • Dimensions: 240 × 320 px
  • Background: White
  • Note: Recent color photo, no cap/sunglasses.

✍️ Railway Signature

  • File Size: 10 KB – 40 KB
  • Dimensions: 140 × 60 px
  • Format: JPG/JPEG
  • Note: Black ink, running signature (not capital).

🏦 Banking Exams (IBPS / SBI / Federal)

📸 Banking Photograph

  • File Size: 20 KB – 50 KB
  • Dimensions: 200 × 230 px
  • Background: White/light-colored
  • Note: Neutral expression, no spectacles/caps glare.

✍️ Banking Signature

  • File Size: 10 KB – 20 KB
  • Dimensions: 140 × 60 px
  • Note: Black ink. Must NOT be in CAPITAL LETTERS.

🖼️ Left Thumb

  • File Size: 20 KB – 50 KB
  • Dimensions: 240 × 240 px
  • Note: Blue or Black ink on white paper.

📝 Declaration

  • File Size: 50 KB – 100 KB
  • Dimensions: 800 × 400 px
  • Note: English text, white paper with black ink.

🏛️ State-Level Exams

📸 State Photograph

  • File Size: 50 KB – 100 KB
  • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm
  • Background: White or light-colored

✍️ State Signature

  • File Size: 20 KB – 50 KB
  • Dimensions: ~3.5 cm × 1.5 cm
  • Note: Black or dark blue ink on white paper.
